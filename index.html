drop the pin. spot the liar.

a map-based social deduction game · play on your own device

HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to Geopostor! A social deduction map game by Jake Juip.

HOW TO SET IT UP

  • The host/moderator needs to create a room/lobby. They will receive a code, and be automatically inserted into the room/lobby.
  • The players should press "join room" and enter this room code and a player name. Press enter room to join.
  • The host/moderator is now able to change some settings. The players will be able to see a read version/mirror of the settings to observe with the host/moderator picks.

HOW TO PLAY

  • You need locations, three or more players, and one should be a moderator.
  • If you are the imposter, you will be greeted with a RED screen, don't let anyone see! this screen will tell you the category, but not the specific prompt word. Choose a location associated with the category carefully to blend in.
  • If you are a player, you'll be greeted with a GREEN screen. Don't let anyone see! Choose a location associated with the prompt, but don't be too obvious!
  • Use a combination of the map and the search bar to drop a pin.
  • When your location is locked in, press SUBMIT PIN at the bottom of the screen. You can change it until all players have submitted.
  • When everyone has submitted, you will advance to an IRL discussion period. click on player names and talk to root out the Geopostor.
  • After 30 seconds of discussion the host/moderator will be prompted to open a vote with a green button.
  • VOTE!
  • This gameplay loop will continue until the Geoposters are all voted off, or the players are all voted off.
